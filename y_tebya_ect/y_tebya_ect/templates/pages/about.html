Why 'У тебя есть'?

As children, we play games to learn what things are called, like Go Fish.

So, I thought, why not practice Russian with a game? У тебя есть is exactly that, a game for English speakers looking to practice their Russian.

Since 'У тебя есть' means 'Do you have' it made sense to make a game that takes the format of Go Fish. You ask for something and the other player replies truthfully.
